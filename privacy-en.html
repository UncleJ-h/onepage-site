Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2026

One Page collects no data.
It's that simple.

What We Collect

Nothing.

One Page is a fully local application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

Data Storage

All data in One Page is stored locally on your device:

  • Favorite cards — stored in your device's local storage
  • Purchase status — managed by Apple's App Store

We cannot access this data and do not attempt to.

Network Connections

One Page does not require an internet connection to function. The only network activity is:

  • App Store purchase verification (handled by Apple)
  • Restore purchases feature (handled by Apple)

These network requests are handled by Apple's system frameworks. We do not participate in or monitor them.

Third-Party Services

One Page contains no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising frameworks.

We do not use:

  • Analytics services (such as Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Crash reporting services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media SDKs

Accounts

One Page does not require account creation and does not support account features. No login, no registration, no user profiles.

Children's Privacy

One Page does not collect data from anyone, including children. The app content is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the updated version on this page. Since we collect no data, there's really nothing to change.

In Summary

  • No personal information collected
  • No user behavior tracking
  • No internet connection required
  • No third-party services
  • Your data stays on your device
